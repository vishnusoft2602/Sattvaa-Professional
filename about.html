About Sattvaa

Sattvaa is a purified drinking water brand built on principles of clarity, health, and sustainability. Our multi-stage purification process ensures water that is safe, lightly mineralized for taste, and packaged responsibly.

Our Process

  1. Source screening and pre-filtration
  2. Reverse osmosis and membrane filtration
  3. UV & ozonation for disinfection
  4. Mineral balancing and quality testing
  5. Bottling in recyclable packaging

Quality & Certifications

We follow strict hygiene and testing guidelines and maintain records for traceability. Our products are regularly tested to meet industry standards.
